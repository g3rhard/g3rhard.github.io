## TL;DR

* **macOS:** Shifted ‚Üí Caps toggles recent app; Caps Lock itself moves to **double-Shift**.

## macOS: Shifted (keep Caps via double-Shift)

1. Install and open **Shifted**.
2. Set **Action ‚Üí Previous Application** (acts like a quick Cmd+Tab).
3. Ensure **Caps Lock via double-Shift** is enabled, so you still have real CAPS when needed.

## Windows: PowerToys ‚Üí Caps Lock triggers Alt+Tab

Goal: **single press of Caps = Alt+Tab once** (jump to previous app).

1. Open **PowerToys ‚Üí Keyboard Manager**.
2. **Remap a key ‚Üí Add**

   * Key: **Caps Lock**
   * Mapped to: **Shortcut** ‚Üí press **Alt** + **Tab**
   * Save.
3. (Optional) Still want CAPS sometimes? Add another mapping:

   * **Shortcut: Shift + Caps Lock ‚Üí Caps Lock** (toggles CAPS when you hold Shift).

> Note: This one-tap shortcut **invokes Alt+Tab once**.
